What is OcuLife?

OcuLife is a screen protector. But it not only protects your screens with new biodegradable technology, it protects your eyes. Humans eyes are one
of the most important parts of our bodies, and imagining a life without vision is the stuff of horror. Using our screen protectors, you will be able to
protect your eyes in the most hassle-free and easy way possible.

How it works

When you apply the screen protector, it not only protects the screen with new technology that protects the screen like never before, but it also
protects your eyes using invisible layers of blue light filter biodegradable plastics. Research shows this technology will decrease the effects of
screens on your eyes by 30, depending on the person. That means, if you don't use our technology you can lose your eyesight by 60 years old,
but with it you can increase it by an extra 18 years!
